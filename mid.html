Midnights Album


Midnights 4 Album Versions

Taylor Swift releases four different Midnight album versions on October 21, 2022. These includes: Moonstone Blue Edition, Jade Green Edition, Blood Moon Edition and Mahogany Edition. She also releases the vinyl edition of each covers.

The albums actually forms into a wall clock, when assembled, holds 4 vinyl albums. Includes four walnut wood shelves, each engraved with one word from the phrase ‘Meet me at midnight’. Comes with brass metal clock center piece with 2 wooden hands that each have Taylor Swift printed in brass ink.”


Midnights Special Edition Album

Taylor Swift releases one special lavender edition album only in Target stores in US. It includes 3 extra tracks namely:
