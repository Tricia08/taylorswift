Meet Taylor Alison Swift

One of the Best Selling Country Pop Musicians of All Time


More About Taylor


Taylor is a American singer-songwriter. Born in West Reading, Pennsylvania, Swift moved to Nashville at age 14 to become a country artist.

Honored with titles such as Artist of the Decade and Woman of the Decade, Swift is an advocate for artists' rights and women's empowerment. Her music is credited with influencing a generation of singer-songwriters.

First Released Single

It was on June 2006 when Taylor released her lead single, "Tim McGraw", which Swift and her mother helped promote by sending copies of the CD single to country radio stations.[46] As there were not enough furniture at the label yet, they would sit on the floor to do so.

First Ever Song Written

Taylor Swift first ever song written was called "Lucky You". She wrote it when she was 12 years old after a repairman who came to her house taught her to play three chords.

Song's Inspirations

Taylor loves to write and sing songs about her experiences and relationships. Most of her songs are based on her crushes,love life, life experiences and ex-boyfriends; either good memories and heartbreaks.
